Perching Landing Gear

Landing gear that enables drones to take off and land on anything from tree branches to telephone wires. 
I did all of the early design work on the fingers, as well as much of the project management for the other sub-systems.

Blind Sailing

Sailing is a wonderful pastime that should be able to be enjoyed by all. We are working to create a system to enable those without sight to sail.
Our team's job was to outfit our O'Day 20 with our sensor suite that can give live feedback to the skipper all data needed to race the boat without the aid of sighted guides.

Otterly Adorable

(Disclaimer: I did not come up with the name). The Transporter is the very first Olin large team project. The project is to design a toy, game, and iteration that will be experienced and judged by a group of fourth graders at the end of the semester. 
My group designed a robotic Otter that could swim along the surface of a pool. 

Laser Harp

Through the use of a speaker, hysteretic oscillator, a couple of photo-resistors, and two lasers, my partner and I built a five-tone (somewhat painful to listen to) laser harp. (sound)
